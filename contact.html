ALAN TUPPER

As an artist, storyteller, and habitual world-builder, Alan spends a lot of time jotting down ideas. He's worked on mutiple products including Out There (IFC), The Awesomes (Hulu), and transmedia projects for Toonix and The Electric Company (Cartoon Network Latin America, PBS respectively). His work on Kamikaze includes world and story development, background design, character design, and writing. The team's go-to guy for all things science and tech related, Alan is a sci-fi geek, virtual world enthusiast, and feared where ever coffee products are sold.

CARRIE TUPPER

A freelance artist committed to the craft of storytelling, Carrie has put her passion for animation and story to use across multiple mediums. She has been central to the design and development of the narrative, characters, and world of Kamikaze. Throughout the process Carrie filled art director, writer and producer roles and acted as team coordinator, all while producing finalized character illustrations. She is a Photoshop wizard, voracious researcher, herder of corgis, and devoted scholar of storytelling.

HAVANA NGUYEN

Though adept at marketing, Havana's natural talents flourish in her positions as a freelance artist and graphic designer. Havana is an Illustrator ninja, ex-introvert, animation geek and the team's only fashionista.
