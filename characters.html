CHARACTERS

MARKESHA NIN

Up walls, down alleys, or across rooftops, Markesha moves like a lightning-struck rabbit.

Her fleet feet are only outpaced by her biting wit. Whether physical or emotional, battle is not her forte - she’s a runner, not a fighter. Constant movement to make ends meet keeps her body strong, her mind clear, and her heart from breaking.

The memory of her absent mother is forever at hand, driving Markesha toward a destiny she cannot imagine.

KAMIKAZE

Markesha has made a life of running away, but as Kamikaze she is a reckless force of nature, taking risks and fighting wars she never could without a mask.

While Kamikaze's anti-grav pack may appear to let her fly, it really only allows for higher jumps, further leaps, faster sprints and slower falls. The pack's power must be vented on a regular basis, or it could overload taking Kamikaze and the entire city with it.

Kamikaze's gloves serve dual roles: power vents and high-voltage taser. She uses these gloves to short out low-insulation computers or low-intelligence assailants, but keeping them away from water is a MUST.

TOSHI NIN

Though blind, Toshi Nin strives to meet every sunrise with a smile no matter how much self-delusion it takes.

His daughter, Markesha is the source of his greatest joy and his deepest fear. Broken down by a lifetime in Outer Trinity, compliance is his only way of staying safe. But Toshi hides a secret talent that may allow him to protect the only family he has left.

ORSON STYKES

To Orson you're either an asset or you're dead. Leader of the rouge sabotage unit Markesha works for, he's the brains behind Kamikaze's kinetic brawn.

Orson is determined to force a regime change in the city-state they call home. Beneath his stoic facade is a laser focused, two-faced strategist bent on advancing his own vengeful agenda by any means necessary.

LINK SOHN

Link is a legendary hacker...in his own mind. If his work ethic matched his talent and ego, he'd be the most sought after hacker in the city.

In the past he settled for working odd jobs while dreaming of the glamor and prestige awaiting him once he “made it.” Now in the big leagues, Link hides a fear of failure behind a lot of swagger and bravado. He's good, but he's in over his head, and he'll die before he admits it.

AUDREY DALMA

It's hard to imagine Audrey as a world class scientific prodigy, but without her inventions, Kamikaze would be tethered firmly to the ground.

Audrey's revolutionary inventions have caused her heartbreak in the past, making her cautious about who she shares her secrets with. Her caring nature makes her the heart of the team, just don't ask too many questions.

CLAY "TORO" BLACKTHORNE

If anyone is Sheriff in Outer Trinity it's Toro.

His gang, the Razors, are arguably the most organized and dangerous group of brutes in Trinity. The Razors have survived endless turf wars through Toro's ferocity and leadership. Once a believer in justice, he now relishes the power of uncontested authority.

IRIS

As much of an enigma as the company she represents, Iris is the sole representative of a foreign power house. Implacable and timeless to the outside world, she would give anything to leave it all behind, but the only way out may cost Iris her life.

JUILAN FOSTER

Julian Foster is an outlaw grower...of vegetables. He's a giant, but those massive hands are better at growing plants than beating punks.

Julian sells his black market food in an attempt to keep the only rebellion in the city from crumbling under it's fractured leadership. A past steeped in shame has left Julian unwilling to take the reins. But Julian has the potential to become a great leader...if he could only find his backbone.

FLARE

If hands need to get dirty and they can't be yours, Flare is the woman to call.

Causing chaos and mayhem wherever she goes, her reputation for getting the job done is flawless. Flare is Kamikaze's most unpredictable and provocative nemesis. To Orson...she's his greatest threat.
